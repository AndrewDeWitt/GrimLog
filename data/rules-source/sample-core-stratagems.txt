# Sample Core Stratagems for Testing
# This file contains a few example stratagems to test the Strategic Assistant

FIRE OVERWATCH (2 CP)
Category: Battle Tactic
When: Opponent's Movement or Charge phase - After enemy unit declares charge
Target: One unit from your army that has been declared as a target of that charge
Effect: That unit can fire Overwatch. Each time a model in that unit makes a ranged attack, an unmodified Hit roll of 6 scores a Critical Hit.
Keywords: Any unit
Restriction: once_per_turn

COUNTER-OFFENSIVE (2 CP)
Category: Battle Tactic
When: Fight phase - After an enemy unit has fought
Target: One unit from your army that is within Engagement Range of one or more enemy units and has not yet been selected to fight this phase
Effect: That unit can fight next, before any other units that have not yet fought this phase.
Keywords: Any unit
Restriction: once_per_turn

HEROIC INTERVENTION (1 CP)
Category: Battle Tactic
When: Charge phase - After an enemy unit ends a Charge move
Target: One CHARACTER unit from your army that is within 6" of that enemy unit and not already within Engagement Range
Effect: That CHARACTER unit can make a Heroic Intervention move of up to 6" towards that enemy unit.
Keywords: CHARACTER
Restriction: none

INSANE BRAVERY (1 CP)
Category: Epic Deed
When: Command phase - Start of phase
Target: One BATTLE-SHOCKED unit from your army
Effect: That unit is no longer BATTLE-SHOCKED.
Keywords: Any unit
Restriction: none

COMMAND RE-ROLL (1 CP)
Category: Strategic Ploy
When: Any phase - Before or after making a single dice roll
Target: One unit from your army
Effect: You can re-roll one Hit roll, one Wound roll, one Damage roll, one saving throw, one Advance roll or one Charge roll.
Keywords: Any unit
Restriction: none

EMERGENCY DISEMBARKATION (1 CP)
Category: Strategic Ploy
When: Any phase - When a TRANSPORT from your army is destroyed
Target: Units embarked within that TRANSPORT
Effect: Set up each unit wholly within 6" of the destroyed TRANSPORT and not within Engagement Range of enemy units. Each model in those units must take a Dangerous Terrain test (roll one D6: on a 1, that model is destroyed).
Keywords: TRANSPORT
Restriction: none

GO TO GROUND (1 CP)
Category: Battle Tactic
When: Opponent's Shooting phase - Start of phase
Target: One INFANTRY unit from your army
Effect: Until the end of the phase, models in that unit have the Benefit of Cover against ranged attacks. In addition, that unit is Battle-shocked until the end of your next Command phase.
Keywords: INFANTRY
Restriction: once_per_turn

RAPID INGRESS (1 CP)
Category: Strategic Ploy
When: Your Movement phase or Opponent's Movement phase - End of phase
Target: One INFANTRY unit from your army that is in Reserves
Effect: Set up that unit anywhere on the battlefield that is more than 9" from all enemy models.
Keywords: INFANTRY
Restriction: once_per_turn

SMOKESCREEN (1 CP)
Category: Battle Tactic
When: Opponent's Shooting phase - After an enemy unit has selected targets
Target: One VEHICLE unit from your army that was selected as a target
Effect: Until the end of the phase, models in that unit have the Benefit of Cover against ranged attacks.
Keywords: VEHICLE
Restriction: once_per_turn

TANK SHOCK (1 CP)
Category: Strategic Ploy
When: Your Charge phase - Start of phase
Target: One VEHICLE unit from your army
Effect: Until the end of the phase, after that unit ends a Charge move, select one enemy unit within Engagement Range and roll one D6 for each model in that enemy unit. For each 5+, that enemy unit suffers 1 mortal wound.
Keywords: VEHICLE
Restriction: none
